About Seima

Seima is a smart financial management application developed by a team of FPT University students. We understand that personal and group financial management is a significant challenge in modern life.

With a mission to help people better control their financial situation, Seima provides powerful and easy-to-use tools for tracking expenses, managing budgets, and collaborating with family and friends.

The application is designed with a user-friendly interface, advanced OCR features, and secure cloud data synchronization capabilities, ensuring the best experience for users.
